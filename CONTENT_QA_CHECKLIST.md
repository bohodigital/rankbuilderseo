# Rank Builder SEO Content QA Checklist

- clear reader and purpose
- short answer near top
- official/primary sources checked first
- factual claims sourced
- facts separated from inference/opinion
- verification steps included
- cost/effort notes included where relevant
- internal links included
- outbound links included where useful
- no fake authority
- no service advertising
- no ranking guarantees
- no unsupported provider accusation
- no AI-invented facts
- metadata complete
- sitemap/RSS regenerated
- validation passes
