## Short answer

Rank Builder SEO is an independent SEO research lab and buyer-defense publication for people who want source notes, verification steps, pricing clarity, and public experiment logs instead of ritual language and sales theater.

## Why it matters

SEO advice often collapses several different jobs into one blur: education, sales, reporting, provider evaluation, and technical implementation. Rank Builder separates those jobs so readers can inspect claims, compare methods, and decide what deserves trust.

## Methodology

This site publishes from a source-first standard. Drafts stay private. Published pages are expected to separate fact from inference, include clear limits, and avoid unsupported claims about providers, audits, and outcomes.
