## Short answer

Rank Builder SEO is designed as a static publication. It does not need user accounts, comments, or lead forms to be readable, but hosting and analytics providers may still process routine operational data when someone loads a page.

## Why it matters

Readers should not have to guess whether an editorial page is doubling as a hidden intake funnel. This site is meant to work without account creation or contact capture built into the page shell.

## How to verify

- Review the page source for the site shell and confirm that the publication does not depend on a login or form workflow.
- Check the deployment platform and analytics tooling that are enabled for the production site.
- Read the host and analytics provider policies for the data they process in their own systems.

## Sources and notes

- The deployment target is Cloudflare Pages, which has its own platform-level documentation and policies.
- If Cloudflare Web Analytics or similar platform tooling is enabled, Cloudflare may process visit data under its own terms.
- This page should be revised if new capture, embeds, or third-party scripts are added later.
