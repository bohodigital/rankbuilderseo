## Three different records

Search Console, web analytics, and your CRM answer different questions. Combining them is useful. Pretending they are interchangeable creates fake certainty.

- Search Console: queries, pages, clicks, impressions

- Analytics: configured events and sessions

- CRM or sales system: qualified leads and revenue

## Expect mismatches

Dates, time zones, attribution, consent, filters, and identity rules differ. Small discrepancies are normal; unexplained large ones deserve investigation.

## Design the decision first

Collect the smallest set of signals that answer a real operating question. More events and dashboards do not automatically create better decisions.
