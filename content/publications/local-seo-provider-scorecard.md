## Ownership before optimization

The business should control its domain, profiles, analytics, advertising accounts, call tracking, and core content. Providers can have delegated access without becoming the owner.

## Evidence in the actual market

Ask how the provider evaluates location, service area, competition, review patterns, local landing pages, and map visibility for your specific business.

- Named local competitors

- Representative non-branded queries

- Lead-quality definitions

- Profile change log

## A scorecard that survives procurement

Weight access, implementation responsibility, reporting clarity, correction policy, and contract exit terms. Treat case studies as context, not transferable guarantees.
