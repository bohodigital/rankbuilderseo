## Define the operating model

Strategy, implementation, content production, digital PR, local profile management, and reporting are different jobs. A price without a delivery model is not comparable.

- Who makes changes?

- Who owns the accounts?

- How much content is included?

- What triggers a re-scope?

## Find the hidden dependencies

A proposal may assume that your developers ship fixes, your team supplies subject expertise, or your approvals arrive in days. Those assumptions affect both cost and likely progress.

## Demand useful reporting

Reporting should connect work to observable changes, search visibility, qualified actions, and remaining uncertainty. Activity counts alone reward motion rather than outcomes.

## Contract questions

Clarify cancellation, access handoff, content ownership, historical data, third-party fees, and the disposition of accounts when the relationship ends.
