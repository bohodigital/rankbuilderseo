Provider Review Methodology

How Rank Builder SEO evaluates SEO provider claims without inventing authority or certainty.

Published July 01, 2026 | Updated July 01, 2026

Short answer

Provider reviews on Rank Builder should be explicit about scope, evidence, and missing information. They are meant to help readers inspect provider claims, not to manufacture rankings or pretend the site reviewed things it did not review.

Methodology

Provider reviews should identify:

  • what public materials were reviewed
  • whether pricing was visible
  • whether deliverables were concrete
  • whether proof of execution existed
  • where evidence was missing
  • where conclusions remain provisional

Why it matters

Provider review pages become dishonest quickly when they flatten different service models into one leaderboard. Buyers need context, not decorative certainty.

Sources and notes

  • Review pages should link back to the citation standard when evidence quality becomes part of the analysis.
  • Named-company statements should stay factual and attributable.
