Audit Review Methodology

How Rank Builder SEO reviews SEO audits for scope, proof, clarity, and buyer usefulness.

Published July 01, 2026 | Updated July 01, 2026

Short answer

Audit reviews should evaluate whether an SEO audit is technically grounded, scoped clearly, and useful to the buyer. They should not confuse long documents with good analysis.

Methodology

Audit reviews should show:

  • what inputs were available
  • whether the findings were reproducible
  • whether evidence was attached
  • whether prioritization made sense
  • where the audit drifted into vague advice

Why it matters

Audits are often sold as authority objects. Buyers need a way to separate reproducible findings from generic problem lists and expensive formatting.

Sources and notes

  • The review should distinguish disagreement from missing proof.
  • If an audit cannot be published in full, the limits of the review should be stated plainly.
