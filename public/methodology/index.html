Methodology

The standards Rank Builder SEO uses for sourcing, reviewing providers, reviewing audits, and correcting pages.

Published July 01, 2026 | Updated July 01, 2026

Short answer

Rank Builder SEO publishes with a source-first method. That means the site explains what was reviewed, what was not reviewed, where claims came from, and how a reader could verify the work independently.

Methodology

The public method is split into three practical standards:

  • Source and Citation Standard
  • Provider Review Methodology
  • Audit Review Methodology

Why it matters

Methods are part of the product. Without them, a review page is just tone and a guide page is just preference wearing a lab coat.

Sources and notes

  • The methodology pages are internal source records for how this publication is meant to operate.
  • Review and experiment pages should link back to the relevant methodology when their claims depend on it.
