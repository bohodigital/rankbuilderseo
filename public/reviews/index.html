How Rank Builder SEO separates provider reviews from audit reviews and keeps both tied to evidence.

Published July 01, 2026 | Updated July 01, 2026

Short answer

Rank Builder keeps provider reviews and audit reviews separate because they answer different questions. One asks whether a provider's public claims hold up. The other asks whether a specific audit was technically useful and well evidenced.

Why it matters

When those categories get blurred, readers end up with decorative rankings instead of review logic. This site keeps the categories separate so the standards stay readable.
